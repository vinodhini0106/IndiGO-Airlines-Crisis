✈️ **IndiGo Airlines Crisis Analytics & Operational Resilience Project**

Completed an end-to-end Business Analytics project analyzing a large-scale aviation operational crisis involving 215,906+ records across 14 CSV and JSON datasets covering flight operations, crew scheduling, FDTL compliance, route revenue, DGCA penalties, weather conditions, and competitor performance.

🔹 **Business Objective**
Investigated whether IndiGo's December 2025 operational crisis was an unpredictable event or a foreseeable outcome of crew-capacity constraints, FDTL Phase II regulations, and night-flight dependency.

🔹 **Key Contributions**
• Designed and executed a complete ETL and data-cleaning pipeline for multi-source aviation datasets.
• Resolved pilot identifier mismatches and created a unified crew master mapping system.
• Standardized timestamps, handled data quality issues, performed schema mapping, deduplication, and currency normalization.
• Conducted root-cause analysis linking FDTL regulations, crew shortages, and flight cancellations.
• Performed competitor benchmarking against Air India, Vistara, SpiceJet, and Akasa Air.
• Built financial impact models to estimate revenue loss, refund liabilities, and regulatory penalties.
• Developed a crew optimization model to determine minimum pilot headcount requirements.
• Designed an FDTL early-warning framework, passenger protection policy, and crisis-management playbook.

📊 **Dashboard Development**
Created an executive Power BI dashboard featuring:
• Cancellation Heatmap
• Crew Availability vs Schedule Analysis
• FDTL Violation Tracker
• Route Revenue Loss Analysis
• Delay Attribution Dashboard

🛠️ **Tools & Technologies**
Excel | SQL | Power BI | Tableau | Python | ETL | Data Cleaning | Data Modeling | Business Intelligence | Data Visualization | Financial Analysis

📈 **Project Outcome**
Delivered a data-driven assessment demonstrating how regulatory constraints, staffing capacity, and operational planning can significantly impact airline performance, while providing actionable recommendations for operational resilience and risk mitigation.
